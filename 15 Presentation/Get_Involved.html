Obtain a seed from the Ancient Trees library

Keep damp in a cool area with some light, but no sun. Do not let the seeds become dry while in the germination period, but make sure not to over-water. When the tree is a few inches tall, it in a larger container with more potting soil.

Choose a site for the redwood. It must be at least 50 feet away from buildings and power lines

Make sure to return to the seedling to water it once a week for the first month
